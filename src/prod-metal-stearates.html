Metal Stearates

Product Description

Metallic stearates are commonly used as lubricants, release agents, and anti-caking agents in a wide variety of industrial and consumer products. In the plastic industry, metallic stearates are used as lubricants in the manufacturing process to reduce friction and wear on equipment, and to improve the flow properties of the plastic resins. They can also be used as a release agent to prevent the plastic from sticking to the molds during the manufacturing process.
