Organotin Stabilzer

Product Description

Organotin stabilizers are chemical compounds that contain tin atoms bonded to organic compounds. They are used as a stabilizer in polyvinyl chloride (PVC) products, which are widely used in various industries such as construction, packaging, and medical devices. They help to improve the thermal stability and mechanical properties of PVC products, as well as to prevent the growth of microorganisms..
