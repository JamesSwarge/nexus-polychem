Suction hose & flexible tubing

Application Description

Our additives are designed to improve the flexibility, strength and weather resistance of the suction hoses and flexible tubes, making them suitable for use in harsh environments. They also improve the processability of PVC, making it easier to produce hoses and tubes with tight tolerances.

Our additives are specially formulated to enhance the physical and chemical properties of PVC, making it more durable and resistant to chemicals, abrasion, and extreme temperatures.
